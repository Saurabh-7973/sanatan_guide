CustomPainter Elements

PeacockPainter — the hero illustration. 7+ radial feather paths each with an eye-spot (concentric ellipse + dot). Body = ellipse. Neck = bezier curve. Head = circle. Crest = 3 thin lines with dot tips. Full ink-drawing style, no fills except subtle dark ground. This painter scales: 200px (empty state) → 40px (search bar prefix icon).
ForestDapplePainter — subtle background. Faint overlapping circles in deep green at varied opacities. Suggest tree canopy.
Search bar prefix — small peacock feather drawn icon (not Material Icons). Single feather path + eye circle.
Suggestion chips — styled as carved-stone labels (deep forest green bg, subtle border). No emojis.
Transition — when user types, peacock animates: tail folds (feathers rotate inward). Results emerge with a leaf-fall stagger animation.

Light Mode

Peacock drawn in deep forest green on warm cream bg. Feather eye-spots in saffron. Forest dapples in warm olive. Chips in cream + forest border.
Why this works: The peacock in Hindu tradition is the vahana of Kartikeya, associated with divine beauty + the destruction of ego. In Naimisharanya, sages gathered to debate dharma. The peacock wandering the forest = knowledge waiting to be summoned. Each feather = a path into wisdom.
