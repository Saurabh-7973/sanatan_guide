Sanatan Guide is a calm, scholarly reading companion for the scriptures of
the sanātana tradition — the Bhagavad Gītā, the four Vedas, the principal
Upaniṣads, the Itihāsas, and more.

Built for daily reading, careful reflection, and an honest conversation
with the source texts.

──────────

WHAT'S INSIDE

📜 35+ classical texts, each in full
   Bhagavad Gītā · all four Vedas · 13 principal Upaniṣads · Yoga Sūtras ·
   Brahma Sūtras · Rāmāyaṇa · Mahābhārata · Bhāgavata Purāṇa · Manusmṛti ·
   Arthaśāstra · Tirukkuṟaḷ · and more.

🕉 Sanskrit · IAST transliteration · English translation
   See every verse in Devanāgarī, romanised with full diacritics, and
   translated by published public-domain translators (Sivananda,
   Prabhupada, Müller, Griffith, others — all attributed).

🪔 Daily verse, every morning
   A new verse waits each day. Schedule a reminder, build a streak, mark
   chapters as you read them.

🙏 Ask the Pandit
   When a verse needs unpacking, ask. Replies cite real verses, link back
   to the source, and stay close to traditional commentary. Powered by
   Google Gemini under the hood — replies are commentary, never verdict.

📚 Read the way you remember
   Bookmark verses. Take personal notes alongside any passage. Save
   AI explanations to your notes. Search across all texts at once.

🎉 Festival calendar with day-of alerts
   Drik Panchang dates for 2026. Optional notification on the morning
   of each major parva. Quiet by default.

🛕 Learning paths
   Short curated modules on dharma, karma yoga, moksha — designed for
   the commute, not the marathon.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

✦ Offline first. The full scripture library lives on your device. No
  account, no login, no cloud sync. Open in airplane mode and read.

✦ No ads. No tracking pixels. Anonymous analytics + crash reports are
  opt-out in one tap.

✦ Heritage-toned design. Lora serif. Saffron + ivory. Devanāgarī font
  pairs that respect the script. No flat-Material chrome.

✦ Open about sources. Every translation lists translator, source URL,
  and license. Public domain wherever possible.

✦ Calm by default. Quiet typography, no notification spam, no
  gamification beyond a gentle daily-read streak.
