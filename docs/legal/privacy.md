# Privacy Policy — Sanatan Guide

*Last updated: 28 May 2026*

Sanatan Guide is a scripture-reading companion for the Bhagavad Gītā, the
Vedas, the Upaniṣads, and other classical texts of the sanātana tradition.
This policy describes what the app collects, why, and what choices you have.
It is written in plain language because privacy disclosures should be
readable.

## What we collect

**Nothing tied to your identity.** Sanatan Guide does not require an
account, an email address, or a phone number. We do not ask for your name,
date of birth, contacts, calendar, photos, or location.

The app stores three kinds of information **on your device only**:

- **Your reading progress** — which verses you have opened, your bookmarks,
  your personal notes, and your daily-reminder preference. This data lives
  in a local SQLite database in the app's private storage and is never
  uploaded.
- **Your settings** — theme, font size, language, opted-in notifications.
- **Cached AI replies** — when you ask the Pandit a question, the most
  recent replies are cached locally so the app feels fast on a slow
  network.

Uninstalling the app removes all of this.

## What is sent off your device

Three optional channels send anonymised information to services we use.
Each one is **off by default for Crashlytics** and follows the OS-level
consent prompt; each can be turned off in **Settings → Privacy** at any
time.

1. **Google Analytics for Firebase** — counts how often a screen is opened
   so we know which features are used. No personally identifying values
   are attached. You can opt out under *Settings → Privacy → Analytics*.
2. **Firebase Crashlytics** — when the app crashes, an anonymised stack
   trace is sent so we can fix the bug. No verse text, notes, or chat
   content is attached. You can opt out under *Settings → Privacy →
   Crash reports*.
3. **Google Gemini API** — when you tap *Ask the Pandit* or *Ask about
   this verse*, your question and the verse text are sent to Google's
   Gemini service to generate a reply. Google's own privacy terms apply
   to this transit (https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms). We never
   attach an identifier to the request.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell any data to anyone.
- We do not run advertising in the app, and we do not embed third-party
  trackers beyond the Firebase services named above.
- We do not back up your notes or reading history to our servers. If you
  uninstall the app, your private notes are lost; please export them
  first if you wish to keep them.

## Notifications

If you opt in to a daily verse reminder or festival alerts, the app
schedules notifications **locally on your device** using Android's
AlarmManager. No notification content passes through our servers.

## Children

Sanatan Guide is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect
information from anyone, so there is nothing additional we collect from
children under the laws of any jurisdiction.

## Your choices

- Turn off Analytics: *Settings → Privacy → Analytics*.
- Turn off Crash reports: *Settings → Privacy → Crash reports*.
- Turn off notifications: at the OS level, or under *Settings → Daily
  reminder*.
- Delete all local data: *Settings → Clear all data*.

## Changes

If this policy changes meaningfully, the *Last updated* line above will
reflect the new date, and the in-app *Privacy* link will point to the
revised text. We will not silently broaden what is collected.
