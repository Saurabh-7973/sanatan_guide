What changed and why

  • Removed the sunrise illustration. Generic "spiritual app" signifier. Replaced with inscriptional typography — the Panchang itself becomes the visual heritage marker.
  • Collapsed three Panchang cards + Upcoming Parva card into one inscriptional line + one festival pill. Same data, half the noise.
  • Verse hero card uses palm-leaf binding metaphor. Two horizontal rules with a central diamond = the binding-string holes that ran through every leaf in a real Sanskrit pothi. Becomes a system motif used in every verse view.
  • Puṣpikā flower glyphs (❁) flank the verse number. Authentic Pallava manuscript chapter-mark — never seen in any other Hindu scripture app.
  • Continue-reading strip surfaced. Was missing entirely. With 8 progress beads (palm-leaf bead-row pattern) showing how far through the chapter you are.
  • Streak surfaced ("2 day streak") on the continue strip. Was buried in Practice tab. Habit-building belongs on home.
  • Greeting personalized + time-aware. "Shubh Prabhat, Saurabh" / "Welcome, Saurabh" for first day.
  • Light mode reads as palm leaf, not just "light theme." Subtle grain texture, sepia-ink text, iron-red accents — the substrate actually feels like aged taal patra.
