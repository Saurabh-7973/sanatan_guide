## What the app is

Sanatan Guide presents:

- Verse-by-verse readings of the Bhagavad Gītā, selected Upaniṣads, the
  Vedas, and other classical texts.
- English and Sanskrit text, transliteration, and short translations.
- An optional **Ask the Pandit** feature that generates explanatory
  prose using a third-party large-language-model API (Google Gemini).
- A daily verse reminder, festival alerts, bookmarks, and personal notes
  that live on your device.

## What the AI feature is — and is not

Replies from *Ask the Pandit* are generated by a machine-learning model.
They are intended as a starting point for reflection, not as a final
authority on doctrine, ritual, or personal conduct. The app provides the
words of the scriptures themselves alongside every AI reply for that
reason.

You should:

- Treat AI explanations as *commentary*, not as *verdict*.
- Consult a qualified ācārya or teacher for matters of practice.
- Cross-check translations against authoritative sources when accuracy
  matters to you.

We do not warrant the AI's replies to be theologically correct, factually
complete, or free of errors.
