The Pandit's Reply
AI · Gemini

The scriptures address grief most directly through Krishna's words to Arjuna, who weeps on the battlefield before fighting his own kinsmen.

Krishna's central teaching: grief arises from misidentifying the eternal self with the perishable body. The wise grieve neither for the living nor for the dead — for the soul is never born and never dies.

The Yoga Sūtras add a practical instruction: cultivate a mind made still by meditation, so sorrow loses its hold.

What changed and why

  • Removed the All / Sanskrit / English / Hindi filter chips at the top. Premature filtering. Users don't know whether they need filters until they have results.
  • Removed the magnifying-glass-in-circle empty state illustration and "Explore the scriptures" headline. Decorative; the search bar already does the empty-state signaling.
  • Replaced single-word chips with intent-organized suggestion rows. "By phrase / by Sanskrit word / by coordinate / by question" — each previewing what kind of search it is. Shows users that search supports multiple intents at once.
  • Recent searches at the top. Returning users see their last few searches with result counts. Standard pattern; was missing.
  • Coordinate detection ("BG 2.47" → direct match). The saffron leaf-thread coord-resolved card slides in above results when a coordinate is recognized. Devanāgarī numerals (२.४७) reinforce the heritage signal — same numeral system used everywhere else in the app.
  • Results grouped by scripture. Each group has a header (Devanāgarī + English + count), and results within use the daṇḍa-marked verse coordinate ‖२·३१‖ established in Verse List. System continuity throughout.
  • Match highlighting on both Sanskrit and English. Matched substring shown in saffron with a subtle saffron background tint — both in Devanāgarī and translation.
  • "Ask the Pandit" CTA at the bottom of the empty state. Same ॐ glyph used for the verse-detail "Explain" feature — intentional system continuity for AI features. Pandit answer view shows curated prose followed by cited verses with daṇḍa coordinates and direct-tap to verse.
  • Search-as-you-type with proper loading state. Three-dot typing indicator on "Searching across 31 scriptures..." — quietly conveys the scale.
  • "View all" affordance on each scripture group. When a group has 87 results in BG, only the top 3 show; "view all" expands. Prevents one common term from dominating the entire result list.
