Signature mechanic

A single oil lamp glows above the folio, its halo pulsing at 4.5 s / breath. The page itself is ruled with a saffron margin — the 0.5 px inner rule that earns the illuminated‑manuscript reference without ever saying the word.

हिन्दी · Hindi
ॐ
कर्म करने में ही तुम्हारा अधिकार है, फल की प्राप्ति में नहीं। तुम कर्मफल के हेतु मत बनो और अकर्म में भी आसक्ति मत रखो।
English
You have the right to action alone, never to its fruits. Let not the fruits of action be your motive, nor let your attachment be to inaction.
