A — Living Temple ✓

Every screen is a chamber of one temple. Onboarding = Gopuram entrance. Home = courtyard at dawn. Search = temple garden with peacock. Verse detail = sanctum sanctorum. Consistent spatial metaphor — you journey deeper as you use the app.

B — Sacred Geography ✓

Each screen draws from a distinct sacred place or legend. Onboarding = river Ganga at sunrise (you step in). Search = Naimisharanya forest where sages debated (peacock wanders). Library = ruins of Nalanda. Verse detail = cave of rishis. Maximum uniqueness — no two screens share an atmosphere.

C — Shared Palette, Unique Energy ✓

Same saffron + cream color language across all screens, but each screen gets its own ornamental element, its own compositional energy, its own cultural reference. Search gets peacock. Festivals gets diyas. Credits gets a carved stone inscription wall. Settings gets an oil-lamp keeper's corner.
